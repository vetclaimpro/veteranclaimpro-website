Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Veteran Claim Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, military service history, and VA claim information necessary to generate documents.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to generate VA claim documents on your behalf. Your data is stored securely in Firebase and is never sold to third parties.

Document Processing

Documents are processed using secure cloud infrastructure. No user data is retained for training purposes.

C-File Storage

If you upload a C-File, you choose whether to keep it stored in your account or delete it immediately after analysis. You may delete it at any time from your Profile settings.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store credit card or payment information.

Data Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab in the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: support@veteranclaimpro.com
